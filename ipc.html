Section 420: Cheating and Dishonestly Inducing Delivery of Property

This section deals with cheating by dishonestly inducing someone to deliver property (money, etc.) or by misleading someone about the nature or quality of goods or services.

Section 406: Criminal Breach of Trust

This section applies when someone dishonestly misappropriates or converts property entrusted to them for their own use.

Section 418: Cheating with Knowledge that Wrongful Loss May Occur

Section 418 applies when a person commits cheating knowing that the wrongful loss will occur to someone whose interest they are bound to protect.

Section 377: Unnatural Offenses

This section addresses sexual offenses committed against the order of nature, including acts like sodomy or bestiality.

Section 465: Forgery

This section deals with punishment for forgery, i.e., when a person makes a false document or electronic record with the intention of cheating or causing damage.

Section 120B: Criminal Conspiracy

Section 120B addresses criminal conspiracy, where two or more persons conspire to commit a crime.

Section 66C: Identity Theft

Section 66C addresses identity theft and provides punishment for anyone who uses someone else's identity to commit fraud.
